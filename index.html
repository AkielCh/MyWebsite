Carbs

(Carbohydrates)
We all love them.

Carbs, one of the sweetest joys in life.

For centuries humans have been consuming carbs in their diet. Around the world they are seen in all cuisines.

For example:
  • Bread
  • Pasta
  • Rice
  • Potatoes
There are also a lot of foods that people don't realise are high in carbs. These include:
  • Condiments
  • Fruit
  • Some vegetables
  • Honey
Here are my top 5 tips for eating carbs:
  1. Kick off the day with whole grains.
    Have wholegrain cereal which is high in fibre and low in sugar. Or even some good old porridge.
  2. Leave the white, go for the whole grain.
    Try using wholegrain bread as it is usually higher in fibre.
  3. There’s more to life than bread.
    Or just skip the bread and replace it with things like a quinoa or bean salad.
  4. Who wants sugar water?
    Go for the fruit as a whole over it’s juice counterpart as they usually have much more fibre and less sugar.
  5. Bring on the beans.
    Instead of having potatoes with your food, fill the rest of your plate with beans and get that extra hit of protein.
Foods high in carbohydrates are an important part of a healthy diet. Carbohydrates provide the body with glucose, which is converted to energy used to support bodily functions and physical activity.
